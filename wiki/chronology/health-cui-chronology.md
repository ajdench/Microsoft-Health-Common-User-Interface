# Health CUI Chronology

| Date | Event | Evidence |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 2004-11 | Microsoft sample website says collaboration with the NHS in England began through the NHS CUI programme. | `Introduction/PatientSafety.aspx` in toolkit mirror |
| 2007-03-14 | Digital Health reports EMIS intends to incorporate CUI elements into EMIS Web; INPS is also said to be exploring adoption. | Digital Health industry report |
| 2007-09-10 | Public Microsoft launch of Health CUI. | Microsoft Source press release |
| 2007-09-10 | A same-day Digital Health report says NHS adoption is still limited and identifies EMIS as the largest supplier then beginning CUI incorporation. | Digital Health industry report |
| 2008-05-13 | Microsoft announces version 1.3 and 18-month roadmap. | Microsoft Source press release |
| 2008 | System C says Medway Sigma has become the first trust-wide UK product available to standardize on the new CUI. | Investegate supplier statement |
| 2008 | Patient Journey Demonstrator article describes a Silverlight prototype built to exemplify MSCUI guidance and says it increased interest in the programme. | PubMed abstract page for the 2008 article |
| 2009-07-13 | Digital Health reports EMIS and CareWorks as Microsoft partner-award finalists and references an iLink HealthVault/CUI implementation. | Digital Health industry report |
| 2009-11-27 | Digital Health reports Aintree's trust-wide Medway Sigma deployment and says the suite uses significant CUI elements. | Digital Health industry report |
| 2010-01-28 | Example bundled guidance version: Patient Banner `4.0.0.0`. | Bundled PDF corpus |
| 2010-05-28 | CodePlex archive records release `8.2.51.000`, the latest localized toolkit release history point in this repo. | CodePlex archive releases page |
| 2012-02-13 | Indra says Valencia's ABUCASIS modernization adapts Microsoft Health Common User Interface principles. | Indra article |
| 2015-09-14 | EPaCCS requirements still say CUI standards `MUST` be followed for demographic input/display and broader CUI guidance `SHOULD` be followed. | 2015 NHS follow-on requirements PDF |
| 2010 | NHS retrospective sources say nine CUI standards were published. | NHS Digital / NHS Standards Directory |
| 2019-07-04 | NHS Digital deprecates the standards. | NHS Digital deprecation notice |
| 2024 | A published HCI-modelling paper explicitly combines NHS CUI and the NHS Design System for Patient Banner and Patient Name Input work. | PubMed abstract page |
