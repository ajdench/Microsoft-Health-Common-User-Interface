# Medication Line Checklist

## Use this when
Designing or reviewing medication row, list-item, or prescribing-preview display.

## Checklist
- Preserve a consistent medication attribute order across views unless the context requires a justified exception.
- Label dose explicitly and keep dose visually easy to find.
- Wrap long medication information rather than truncating it.
- Keep labels attached to their values when content wraps across lines.
- Preserve visible separation between one medication line and the next.
- Differentiate current and historical medication states clearly.
- Avoid ambiguous abbreviation unless the abbreviation itself is standardized and safe.
- Keep brand/generic relationships legible where both matter for selection or administration.
- Provide a deliberate preview/review step before final prescribing authorization.
