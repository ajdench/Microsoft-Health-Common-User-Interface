## Interpretation
- The best-proven product/deployment line in the repo is now System C / Medway Sigma, especially because it combines a supplier statement with a named trust deployment.
- EMIS has strong evidence of commitment and incorporation intent, and good evidence of real product work, but still weaker proof of exact deployment footprint than System C.
- The Indra / Valencia line is now good evidence of adaptation outside the original NHS England frame, but it remains vendor-authored.
- Several other names from the early Microsoft and Digital Health ecosystem remain at the level of collaboration or recognition rather than fully proven deployment.
