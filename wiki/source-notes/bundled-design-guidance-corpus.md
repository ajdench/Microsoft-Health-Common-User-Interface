## Short summary
This corpus contains 58 localized PDF artefacts: the principal design-guidance documents and one-page crib sheets that supported practical interpretation of the guidance.

## Key extractable insights
- The corpus covers patient banner, patient name, patient identification number, date/time, address, medication line, medications list/views, search and prescribe, adverse drug reaction risks, clinical noting, tables/graphs, filtering/sorting/grouping, timeline view, and terminology topics.
- The documents are detailed enough to support concrete UI behaviour and formatting rules rather than only high-level principles.
- Several guidance documents are versioned in 2010 and explicitly reference related documents to be read in conjunction with them.

## Should this influence modern reinterpretation?
Yes. This is the main primary corpus for extracting historically grounded UI patterns and safety-oriented rules.
