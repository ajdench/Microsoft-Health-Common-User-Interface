## Short summary
The abstract reports that researchers combined NHS CUI guidance with the NHS Design System, finding that Patient Banner and Patient Name Input components could be produced, while also identifying inconsistencies between the two.

## Key extractable insights
- The paper treats CUI as still useful enough to combine with current NHS design guidance.
- It says Patient Banner and Patient Name Input were technically feasible targets for combined design artefacts.
- It identifies tension between older CUI assumptions and newer NHS Design System practice.

## Should this influence modern reinterpretation?
Yes. It is some of the strongest current evidence that moving from historical archive work to modern design-system translation is worthwhile.
