## Short summary
This mirror provides the localized codebase for the Microsoft Health Common User Interface toolkit, including Web, WinForms, and WPF control libraries, sample applications, and the sample website used to present guidance and roadmap content.

## Key extractable insights
- The toolkit spans multiple UI stacks: ASP.NET AJAX/Web Forms, WinForms, and later WPF/Silverlight-oriented code.
- Core clinician-facing controls include patient banner, patient/name/identifier/date/time display and input, medication display components, patient search, graphing, and search/prescribe components.
- The sample website bundles primary design-guidance PDFs and crib sheets, making the mirror a durable local source for historical guidance.

## Should this influence modern reinterpretation?
Yes. It is the strongest local source for understanding what Microsoft and NHS collaborators actually built, not just what they announced.
