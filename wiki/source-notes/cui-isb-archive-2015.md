## Short summary
The archived ISB page frames Common User Interface as a set of standards intended to define a common interface for healthcare IT systems and lists the numbered standards for address, date/time, NHS number, patient banner, patient name, sex/current gender, telephone number, and time display.

## Key extractable insights
- The page links the CUI standards directly to ISB numbers 1500 through 1508.
- It offers additional compliance-assessment materials including a CAPS compliance pack and a compliance checklist.
- It points readers to the broader archived user-interface-guidance page for medications, clinical noting, and other related guidance.

## Should this influence modern reinterpretation?
Yes, but mainly as governance and provenance context. It helps separate formally standardized material from broader advisory design guidance.
