## Short summary
This guidance defines the patient banner as a core patient-identification surface within clinical applications and gives detailed rules for data set, grouping, labeling, alignment, deceased-patient handling, and optional supplementary information.

## Key extractable insights
- The guidance treats the patient banner as a safety-critical composite pattern rather than a decorative page header.
- It mandates a minimum data set, persistent Zone 1 visibility, and distinct handling for deceased-patient context.
- It allows supplementary information such as contact details and allergy propensity, but keeps identification data primary.
