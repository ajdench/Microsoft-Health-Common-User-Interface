## Trustworthiness / caveats
- The strongest evidence here is the localized toolkit source itself.
- The Telerik and GovLoop pages are only secondary corroboration that specific `pjd.mscui.net` URLs were publicly reachable.
- The actual showcase media files and live Silverlight experiences are not preserved in this repo.

## Short summary
The toolkit mirror preserves the structure of the MSCUI showcase branch even though the live site is gone. It names the demonstrators, references missing media files from `www.mscui.net/showacase/media/`, includes a placeholder for the Patient Journey Demonstrator, and shows that the Care Pathways demonstrator was also distributed as an MSI download.

## Key extractable insights
- The showcase branch contained a `Demonstrators.aspx` page and Silverlight `Microsoft.Cui.ShowcaseControls.xap`.
- The localized `Showcase/Media/Readme.txt` names these media files:
  - `MSCUI_Demonstrators_Future_Health_Care.wmv`
  - `MSCUI_Demonstrator_Care_Pathways.wmv`
  - `MSCUI_Demonstrator_Nursing_View.wmv`
  - `MSCUI_Demonstrator_Patient_Admission.wmv`
  - `MSCUI_Demonstrator_Patient_Review.wmv`
  - `MSCUI_Programme.wmv`
- `DemonstratorsPage.xaml` names the showcase experiences:
  - `Patient Journey Demonstrator`
  - `Health: Future Vision`
  - `Pediatric Secondary Care – Patient Admission`
  - `Pediatric Secondary Care – Patient Review`
  - `Secondary Care – 'Nursing Now' View`
  - `Secondary Care – Care Pathways`
- The toolkit includes a content reference to `Showcase/Download/Microsoft Health CUI Care Pathways Demonstrator.msi`.
- `PatientJourneyDemonstrator.htm` explicitly says the Patient Journey Demonstrator was not available as code download.
- `ViewVideo.xaml.cs` shows the showcase expected a separate Patient Journey Demonstrator directory or absolute URL.
- The Telerik forum captures a 2010 third-party statement that `http://pjd.mscui.net/PrimaryCare.htm` was reachable.
- The GovLoop archive captures a 2010 public share of `http://pjd.mscui.net/default.htm`.
