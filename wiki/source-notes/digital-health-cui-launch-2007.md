## Short summary
This report covers the public international launch of Health CUI, but the most useful part is its caution. It says NHS adoption was still limited at launch time and identifies EMIS as the largest supplier then beginning to incorporate CUI into products.

## Key extractable insights
- The article says adoption within the NHS was still limited in September 2007.
- It says EMIS was the largest supplier then beginning to incorporate CUI elements.
- It frames CUI as a free toolkit plus guidance package rather than a mandatory standard at launch.

## Should this influence modern reinterpretation?
Yes. It is important guardrail evidence against overstating immediate NHS-wide uptake.
