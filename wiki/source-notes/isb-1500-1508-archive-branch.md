## Trustworthiness / caveats
- These artefacts come from the official archived ISB site and are the strongest recovered bridge between Health CUI design guidance and formal standards release material.
- The standard-detail pages are concise summaries, not substitutes for the underlying design guides.
- The DSCN PDFs are formal release artefacts, but later NHS guidance says the standards are now deprecated and should not be relied on unchanged.

## Short summary
This archive branch localizes the formal ISB layer for the CUI family: individual standard pages, linked documentation pages, and the DSCN PDFs for ISB 1500-1508. Together they show how the patient-identification standards were framed, approved, and released.

## Key extractable insights
- The ISB pages clearly describe each standard’s scope and applicability, especially around patient demographics display.
- The documentation pages connect each standard to DSCN release material and shared submission, development-approach, and safety-case documents.
- This branch helps distinguish formally approved demographics standards from the broader Health CUI design-guidance corpus for medications, navigation, and decision support.

## Should this influence modern reinterpretation?
Yes, especially for provenance, governance, and understanding which parts of Health CUI were treated as formal standards rather than only advisory design guidance.
