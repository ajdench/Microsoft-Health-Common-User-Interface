## Short summary
This guide documents the CUI programme’s distribution mechanism, showing that the programme operated through a more structured delivery portal than a simple public download page.

## Key extractable insights
- Health CUI had an explicit distribution and support model, including sign-in, registration, and help flows.
- The programme expected organizations to engage with materials through a managed access process.
- This supports the view that Health CUI functioned as a governed programme with operational processes, not just an informal document library.
