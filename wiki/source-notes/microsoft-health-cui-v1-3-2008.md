## Short summary
This press release documents the May 13, 2008 release of Health CUI version 1.3, highlighting substantial guidance updates, an 18-month roadmap, and a shift toward WPF and Silverlight demonstrators.

## Key extractable insights
- Microsoft explicitly highlighted medication management, patient record noting, hand-over of care, navigation, and patient identification as core risk areas.
- The release reported over 1,100 published pieces of UI guidance and several thousand toolkit downloads by that date.
- It frames the product as both guidance and toolkit, not only one or the other.

## Should this influence modern reinterpretation?
Yes, especially for understanding the intended scope of the platform and the historical move from older .NET UI stacks toward richer client technologies.
