## Short summary
This press release marks the public launch of the Microsoft Health Common User Interface on 10 September 2007, after more than two years of collaboration with the NHS in England.

## Key extractable insights
- Microsoft described the initial release as guidance plus software code for patient-critical functions.
- The release explicitly framed consistency, patient safety, clinician effectiveness, and reduced training/support costs as primary goals.
- The press release states the toolkit standardized more than 100 clinical needs and was already in use in the NHS in England.
