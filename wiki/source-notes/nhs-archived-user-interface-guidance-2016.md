## Short summary
This archived NHS Digital page is the clearest recovered index for the later Health CUI guidance corpus. It groups the materials into patient identification, medications management, clinical noting and terminology, handover, navigation, abbreviations, and decision support.

## Key extractable insights
- The page says guidance documents contain criteria that should be followed when designing web-based or standalone applications for healthcare professionals.
- It explicitly links Health CUI guidance to ISB-issued standards and says some standards were mandated for compliance in 2015.
- It exposes downloadable artefacts not present in the toolkit mirror bundle used earlier in the repo, including QIGs, handover documents, keyboard shortcuts, care pathways modelling, a patient-identification ZIP bundle, and a decision-support summary PPT.

## Should this influence modern reinterpretation?
Yes. It should shape provenance and corpus completeness, but not be used as a substitute for the underlying guidance documents themselves.
