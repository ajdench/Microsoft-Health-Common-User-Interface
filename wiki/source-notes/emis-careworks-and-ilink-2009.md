## Short summary
Digital Health reported that EMIS and CareWorks were finalists in Microsoft’s health partner awards, while iLink Systems was said to have implemented a community-based personal health record using HealthVault and Health CUI.

## Key extractable insights
- EMIS Web was framed as a major next-generation product with Microsoft technology backing.
- The article gives explicit evidence of at least one claimed non-UK implementation context through iLink and HealthVault.
- It suggests the programme had enough external traction for Microsoft to use it in partner-recognition activity by mid-2009.

## Should this influence modern reinterpretation?
Moderately. It is more valuable for adoption history than for direct UI design rules.
