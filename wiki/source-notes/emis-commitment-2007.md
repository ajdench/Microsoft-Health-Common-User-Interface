## Trustworthiness / caveats

## Short summary
Digital Health reported in March 2007 that EMIS planned to incorporate CUI elements into EMIS Web, and that INPS was also working with Microsoft on guidance adoption.

## Key extractable insights
- EMIS was presented as the first major GP supplier publicly committing to incorporate elements of the CUI.
- The article ties the programme to interoperability and to practical concerns such as standard date/time handling and patient ID components.
- It also reports Microsoft saying BT, System C, Axis Technologies, Learning Clinic, and INPS were working with the CUI.

## Should this influence modern reinterpretation?
Yes as evidence that the programme reached real suppliers and was not only an internal Microsoft showcase.
