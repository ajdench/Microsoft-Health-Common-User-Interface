## Short summary
This notice records the formal deprecation of the nine Common User Interface standards and explains that they were considered out of date by 2019, while still retaining residual value in some data elements.

## Key extractable insights
- Confirms the nine named standards published in 2010.
- Confirms that the deprecated documents remained on their original websites, later transferred to the National Archives.
- Provides authoritative evidence that current teams should not treat the historical standards as still-current normative UI rules.

## Should this influence modern reinterpretation?
Yes. It is essential for separating historically useful concepts from standards that should not be adopted uncritically today.
