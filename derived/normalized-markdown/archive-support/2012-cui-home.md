Welcome to the NHS Common User Interface (CUI) Programme

The primary aim of the Programme is to provide a health specific user interface, built on a solid infrastructure, for every medical practitioner across the NHS in England.  This enables a common look and feel across the five regions of the NHS as prescribed within the NHS plan, and provides components for a common interface to those services provided by the LSPs and ISVs. Such a provision would deliver benefits in line with the strategy of the National Programme by providing:

Increased patient safety

Increased clinical take up of systems

Increased clinician effectiveness and efficiency

Reduced training and support costs
